Pasta with Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce and serve hot.

Classic Beef Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 burger bun
  • Lettuce, tomato, and onion slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Ketchup and mustard

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill or stovetop pan.
  2. Season beef with salt and pepper, then shape into a patty.
  3. Grill the patty for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  4. Toast the bun and assemble the burger with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, ketchup, and mustard.

Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)
  • Oregano and basil for seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with oregano and basil, slice, and serve.
